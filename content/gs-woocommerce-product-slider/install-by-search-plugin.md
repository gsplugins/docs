---
title: 'Install by Search Plugin'
weight: 1
---

- In your Admin, go to menu Plugins & Add New
- Search **GS Products Slider for WooCommerce**
- Click to install
- Activate the plugin
- GS Products Slider for WooCommerce will available under GS Plugins & Woo Product Slider
