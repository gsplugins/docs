---
title: 'Install by Search Plugin'
date: '2019-11-07T06:48:06+00:00'
weight: 1
---


- In your Admin, go to menu Plugins > Add New
- Search WordPress Posts Grid
- Click to install
- Activate the plugin
- Posts Grid settings will found - GS Plugins > Posts Grid Settings
