Go to Testimonials -&gt; All Testimonials to see all the Testimonials you added untill now. Mentioned columns will display here

- Author Name
- Testimonial Categories
- Author Image
- Company
- Designation
- Date

You can **sort** by

- Author Name
- Company
- Date
