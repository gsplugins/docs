---
title: 'Install by Upload Plugin'
date: '2019-11-07T06:34:49+00:00'
weight: 2
---

- Download the latest version of GS Course (.zip file)
- In your Admin, go to menu Plugins > Add New
- Select **Upload Plugin**
- Click on **Choose File**
- Select downloaded <code>gs-course-pro-2.0.1.zip</code> & click on **Install Now** button
- Activate the plugin
- GS Course post type will show below Posts / Pages
