---
title: 'Install by Search Plugin'
date: '2019-11-06T12:12:18+00:00'
weight: 1
---

- In your Admin, go to menu Plugins & Add New
- Search GS Dribbble Portfolio
- Click to install
- Activate the plugin
- GS Dribbble Portfolio will available under GS Plugins & Dribbble Settings
