Available fields to add info:

- Coach Name
- Coach Details
- Profession
- Experience
- Education
- Skills
- Address
- State/City
- Country
- Contact Number
- Schedule Time
- Availability
- Fee
- Review
- Student’s Rating
- Linkedin
- Twitter
- Facebook
- Google+
- Youtube
- Personal Site
- Course Link
- Coach Group/Category
- Topics Speciality
- Certified On
- Coach Image
- Coach’s Certificate (Multiple Certificates)
