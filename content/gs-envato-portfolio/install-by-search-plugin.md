---
title: 'Install by Search Plugin'
date: '2019-11-07T06:11:00+00:00'
weight: 1
---

- In your Admin, go to menu Plugins > Add New
- Search GS Envato Portfolio
- Click to install
- Activate the plugin
- GS Envato Portfolio will available under GS Plugins > Envato Settings
